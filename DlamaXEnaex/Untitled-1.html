Welcome to AgriDay 2025

AgriDay 2025 celebrates the achievements of learners in the Animal and Plant Production Learnership Programs, empowering youth with hands-on agricultural and entrepreneurial skills. It’s a gathering where learners, stakeholders, and industry leaders connect to shape a greener, inclusive future for agriculture.
